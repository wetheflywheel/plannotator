## Decisions made
- **AppInner pattern over entry-point wrapping**: AutoReviewProvider wraps inside App.tsx (not in apps/hook/index.tsx or apps/portal/index.tsx) so both entry points get the provider automatically
- **Atomic begin_countdown action**: Combined phase+seconds dispatch to prevent race condition where "countdown hit 0" transition fires between two separate dispatches
- **planBeforeApplyRef**: Snapshots plan at deliberation start (not at apply time) so diff stats compare the correct baseline after onPlanRevised updates planRef
- **Banner gating**: ReviewMetaBanner hidden when drawer is open since drawer's Models section is a superset; reappears when drawer is dismissed
